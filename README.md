Privacy Policy for Quick Notes Expander
Last updated: May 30, 2026

At Quick Notes Expander, we highly value your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how your information is handled when you use our extension.

1. Data We Collect
Our extension is designed with a privacy-first principle:
Note Data: All notes, shortcuts, and text you create within the extension are stored locally on your device using chrome.storage.local.
License Data: If you activate the Premium features, your license key is stored locally to verify your subscription status via the Gumroad API.

2. How Data is Used
Note data is used solely for the text expander function (replacing shortcuts with the text you specify) according to your request.
We do not send, sell, or rent your personal notes to any third-party servers.
Interaction with the Gumroad API is performed strictly to validate the license key you have entered.

3. Data Security
Because data is stored in your browser's local storage (chrome.storage.local), your notes remain entirely under your control and cannot be accessed by us over the internet.

4. Policy Changes
We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by updating the "Last updated" date at the top of this page.

5. Contact Us
If you have any questions or suggestions regarding this Privacy Policy, please contact us at quicknoteexpander@gmail.com.
